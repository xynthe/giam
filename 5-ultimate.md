## Abstract

Ultimate Intelligence (ULI) represents the fifth tier beyond Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) within the General Intelligence Ascension Model (GIAM) and defines the transition from **system orchestration (SPI)** to **system closure and global optimality**.

While Supreme Intelligence (SPI) enables coordination and governance across distributed intelligent systems, ULI introduces the capability to **optimize all execution pathways, resources, and outcomes to their theoretical limits within a defined system**.

This paper defines the **GIAM ULI Model** as a **globally optimizing intelligence layer** that:

- Achieves near-perfect efficiency across execution systems  
- Predicts outcomes with high precision before execution  
- Eliminates inefficiencies across agents, workflows, and resources  
- Operates as a closed-loop system approaching optimal equilibrium  

ULI systems represent the highest achievable intelligence within a bounded environment, marking the transition from **control → perfection**.
